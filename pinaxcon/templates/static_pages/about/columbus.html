Travel and Lodging

We weren't able to secure a hotel block this year so we do not have an official hotel.

The Blackwell is within walking distance and may have rooms available even though there is no block. A number of other hotels are within a 15-min drive: Find hotels near The Ohio Union.

When you get here, you're sure to find a nice place to stay that fits your budget. You can find a range of options, and some special discounts, available on the Where to Stay page.
